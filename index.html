1 Information We Collect

SkyPDF is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not create accounts, require login, or collect personally identifiable information. Below is what the app does and does not access:

📷

Camera Data

Used only to capture document images in real-time. Images are processed locally and never uploaded to any server.

🖼️

Gallery / Storage

Accessed only when you explicitly choose to import a photo or save a PDF. We never browse or copy your files without action.

📄

PDF Files

Created and stored locally on your device. You choose where to save them. We have no access to their contents.

🚫

No Personal Data

We do not collect name, email, location, contacts, phone number, or any personal identifier.

We may collect anonymous, non-identifiable crash reports and app diagnostics (if you have enabled this in Android settings) to help improve app stability. These reports contain no personal information and cannot be traced back to you.

2 How We Use Your Information

The limited data we interact with is used exclusively to provide the core functionality of the App:

  • Scanning documents using the device camera and converting them to PDF format
  • Importing images from your gallery to create PDF files
  • Saving generated PDF files to your chosen device storage location
  • Displaying recently created files within the App for easy access
  • Sharing PDF files via apps you choose (email, messaging, cloud storage)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics tracking, or any commercial purpose beyond delivering the above features.

3 Camera & Storage Permissions

SkyPDF requests the following Android permissions, all of which are used solely for in-app functionality:

  • CAMERA — Required to capture document photos for scanning. Used only when you open the scanner screen.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES — Required to import images from your device gallery for PDF creation.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — Required on older Android versions to save PDF files to your device.
  • READ_MEDIA_VISUAL_USER_SELECTED — On Android 14+, allows targeted media access for image import.
All permissions are requested at the time of use and can be revoked at any time through your device's Settings → Apps → SkyPDF → Permissions. Revoking a permission will disable the related feature but will not affect other app functionality.

4 Data Storage & Security

All files created by SkyPDF are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your documents. No document content, image, or PDF is ever transmitted over the internet by SkyPDF.

  • PDFs are saved to your device's internal storage or SD card, as you choose
  • Recent files data is stored using secure local app storage (SharedPreferences / Room Database)
  • No cloud sync, backup, or remote access is performed by SkyPDF

The security of your documents depends on the security of your device. We recommend using a screen lock and keeping your Android OS updated to protect your local files.

5 Third-Party Services

SkyPDF may include the following third-party components. Each has its own privacy practices:

  • Google Play Services — Used for app distribution and optional in-app updates. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • AndroidX CameraX — A Jetpack library for camera functionality. All processing is on-device with no data transmission.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled) — Collects anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs. No personal data is included. See: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

We do not integrate advertising SDKs, social media trackers, or data broker services into SkyPDF.

6 Children's Privacy

SkyPDF is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal information from any user, the app presents no additional risk to minors.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the address below and we will address the concern promptly.

7 Your Rights & Choices

Because SkyPDF does not collect or store personal data on our end, most traditional data rights (access, portability, erasure) apply locally on your device. You have full control:

  • Delete your PDFs — Use your device's file manager or the App's recent files screen to delete any PDF created by SkyPDF
  • Revoke permissions — Withdraw camera or storage access at any time via Android Settings
  • Uninstall the App — Removing SkyPDF will delete all app data including the local recent files list
  • Opt-out of crash reporting — Disable "Send diagnostic data" in Android Settings → Privacy, or uninstall the App

8 Data Retention

We do not retain any user data on our servers because we do not collect it. Your PDF files exist only on your device for as long as you choose to keep them. If anonymous crash data is collected, it is retained by crash reporting services per their own retention policies and is automatically purged after 90 days.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of SkyPDF after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
